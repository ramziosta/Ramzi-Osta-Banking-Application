How to use this app?
Start by hitting the Home Button and hover and click on the navigation buttons and the register button.

  1. Register a new BadBank account.
    Form validation will guide you through the process.
    Try inputing the wrong information to see form validation.

  2. Login to your account or register a new BadBank account.

  3. When you login; You will land on the Dashboard where you get a summary of your account.
    Choose type of transaction.

  4. Whether you choose deposit or withdraw, the form will guide you through transaction validations.

  5. Check the dashboard to see a history of your transactions.

  6. Check User Data to check your account information.

  7. logout and login with your account email and password.
